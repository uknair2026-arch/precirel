Capabilities

Engineering Excellence

Our facility combines advanced machining systems with exhaustive QA standards to supply precision components globally.

CNC Machining

High-precision multi-axis milling and turning centers. We machine complex components to tight dimensional tolerances (+/- 0.005mm) for aerospace and industrial assemblies.

Cold Heading

High-speed, high-volume production of fasteners and socket products. Cold heading improves grain flow and material strength while reducing waste and procurement costs.

Titanium Fasteners

Aerospace-grade high-strength fasteners manufactured from Titanium Grade 5 (Ti-6Al-4V), Inconel, and custom superalloys. Optimized for high strength-to-weight ratio and corrosion resistance.

Complex Geometries

Conversion of advanced, intricate design blueprints into high-precision custom fittings, connectors, and specialized fasteners that standard catalog items cannot match.

Production

Our Production Process

A rigorous, systematic engineering flow that guarantees dimensional precision and metallurgical integrity.

01

DFM Review

Every engineering drawing undergoes rigorous Design for Manufacturability review, optimizing machining cycles and procurement feasibility.

02

Precision Production

Production runs on state-of-the-art multi-axis CNC machines and cold/hot headers to execute blueprints with tight tolerance control.

03

Rigorous QA

100% inspections via coordinate measuring machines (CMM) and exhaustive metallurgical, tensile, and hardness testing.

04

Global Delivery

Secure industrial packaging, complete materials traceability records (EN 10204 3.1), and rapid logistical dispatch worldwide.
